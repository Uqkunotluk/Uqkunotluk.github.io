    Projects

    The mechanical design project combines my knowledge of materials and mechanics, and for me it enhances my knowledge of mechanical components.

    I4 Engine

    The first item of personal project, inline four-cylinder engine. Such engines are commonly found in four-cylinder automotive internal combustion engines with a displacement of less than 2.0 liters. Because its four cylinders are arranged in a straight line, it is called a coaxial four-cylinder. This project is based on my love for large-scale mechanical structures, so I plan to master the design principles of auto parts within three years, and lay the foundation for future aerospace material design.

    Root Blower

    The Roots-type blower is a positive displacement lobe pump which operates by pumping a fluid with a pair of meshing lobes resembling a set of stretched gears. World war one engineers actually put them on aircraft to force air into the engine, so they get higher altitudes and make enough horsepower to get the airplane up. The roots have three straight lobes and do not touch each other. This is the hardest part of my design because I have to make sure I don't calculate the wrong size.

    Landing Gear

    The third personal design project is the landing gear. The design of the landing gear is the most interesting and the most comprehensive discipline ability test, because the weight of the landing gear must be considered when designing, which involves the content of materials and the landing gear. The effect of size on the hydrodynamics of the aircraft and so on. The rear bracket and cylinder and brake assembly design are also time-consuming parts.

    Intern Projects

    The design and learning process in the factory show that there is a huge gap between reality and theory.

    Design and Material processing

    Truss Manipulator Design

    While steel production relies on machine tool automation, the tasks of gripping and adjusting sheets and distributing finished steel still require steel mill workers to operate in high heat. This is very hard work, so in order to keep workers in a safer environment and work away from hot machine tools, companies have developed a full-line automated production plan. The project I was involved in was mainly to design a large truss manipulator. After on-site measurement and planning in the factory, our team successfully designed the truss manipulator. In it, I was mainly responsible for the design of the lifting beam and the suction cup frame.

    Submarine structure steel Enhancement

    Since the environment in which the submarine performs the task is below the sea surface, the requirements for the steel used in the submarine structure will be very high. The experiments I participated in and successfully published the article were mainly about the improvement of the casting method and heat treatment processing method of submarine steel. The difference in the tensile fracture of the material before and after modification observed by electron microscopy is very significant.

    Physical Vapor Deposition

    Surface Enhancement Method

    Physical vapor deposition (Physical Vapor Deposition, PVD) technology refers to the use of physical methods under vacuum conditions to vaporize the surface of a material source (solid or liquid) into gaseous atoms or molecules, or partially ionize into ions, and deposit certain materials on the surface of the substrate. A technique for special functional thin films, physical vapor deposition is one of the main surface treatment techniques. The experiment I participated in used vacuum sputtering coating technology. When operating the equipment, what impressed me the most was the process of obtaining argon ions by controlling the current. I also found through research that plastics and foams other than metals can be used as targets. . I am very honored to be able to conduct a coating experiment in Taiwan, where chip technology is developed.
